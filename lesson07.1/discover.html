Demographic Data:

Population: In the 2021 Census, the City of Calgary had a population of 1,306,784 residents, representing 30.7% of the 4,262,635 residents in all of Alberta, and 3.5% compared to a population of 36,991,981 in all of Canada. The total population of the Calgary census metropolitan area (CMA) was 1,481,806. Calgary is the largest city in Alberta, and the third-largest municipality and fifth-largest metropolitan area in Canada, as of 2021.

Economic Strength:

Calgary boasts a robust and growing economy. The city benefits from a strategic location, close proximity to major transportation routes, and a skilled workforce. Industries such as technology, healthcare, education, manufacturing, and retail contribute significantly to the local economy, providing abundant job opportunities and driving innovation.


Education:

Calgary takes pride in its excellent educational institutions, from highly regarded public and private schools to prestigious higher education options. The city is home to Clark College, which offers a wide range of academic programs and serves as a hub for lifelong learning.


Outdoor Paradise:

Nature enthusiasts will fall in love with Calgary's scenic wonders. The city is nestled along the banks of the Columbia River, offering breathtaking views and a host of recreational activities. Whether you enjoy hiking, kayaking, or exploring local parks, Calgary's natural beauty provides endless opportunities for adventure and relaxation.


Arts and Culture:

Immerse yourself in Calgary's vibrant arts and cultural scene. The city is home to numerous galleries, theaters, and museums, showcasing a variety of artistic expressions. Residents and visitors alike can enjoy live performances, exhibitions, and community events that celebrate the city's creative spirit.


Calgary is renowned for its high quality of life. The city prides itself on its safe neighborhoods, well-maintained parks, and a strong sense of community. Residents benefit from excellent healthcare facilities, a wide range of recreational amenities, and a thriving local food scene, making Calgary an ideal place to call home.

Come and experience the unique charms of Calgary, Alberta. Whether you're seeking natural beauty, cultural experiences, or a vibrant community, this city has it all. Plan your visit or make Calgary your new home today and discover why it's an exceptional place to live, work, and play.
