Audiences and Scenarios

Target audience:

People who would like to visit the site are adults, interested in getting to know the city either as a tourist or an immigrant. These users seek to know what the city offers in terms of structure, events and attractions.

And the other niche is business owners looking to be more involved in the community, wanting to network with other local entrepreneurs and spread the word about their own business.

Personas:

>

Persona 1 -

Entrepreneur. You want to expand your business, establish partnerships and promote your products or services.

>

Persona 2 -

This individual is migrating and wants to know what the city offers. She wants to know as much as she can about the local people, events, locations, attractions and economy. Would Calgary be a good place to live? How it can help you grow and thrive as a person and as a family. Or just want to spend a few days and in a cozy place with good options.

Scenarios

  • A family coming from another country is looking for a good city to live in.
  • The user comes to the site looking for activities in Calgary.
  • A person is wanting more information about the chamber of commerce but doesn't know how to get it.
  • Someone has started a business and wants to get closer to other entrepreneurs but doesn't have a reference.
  • A member of the Chamber of Commerce looking for when is the next meeting.
