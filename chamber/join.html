Join Our Chamber of Commerce

NON-PROFIT
  • Grow your network by connecting with similar organizations.
  • Show up through our Camera events and promotional channels.
  • Increase your chances of building amazing partnerships.
  • Collaborate on community initiatives and make a positive impact along with other nonprofits.
  • Showcase your non-profit's mission and events through featured listings in our chamber publications and newsletters.
  • Gain access to exclusive workshops and training sessions tailored specifically for non-profit organizations.
ELITE ($CAD 80 / month)
  • Add to the previous benefits:
  • Visibility in our business directory.
  • VIP invitations to business training events.
  • Better visibility for your brand through our social media platforms.
  • Monthly participation in events for the growth of connections with entrepreneurs and professionals in their field.
  • Advertising campaign discounts in our marketing materials.
PREMIER ($CAD 200 / month)
  • Enjoy all the advantages of the Bronze membership, plus:
  • Spotlight your business with enhanced visibility in our directory and on our website.
  • Participate in our prestigious Chamber committees and influence local policies.
  • Showcase your expertise as a guest speaker at Chamber events, positioning yourself as a thought leader in your industry.
  • Receive priority access to sponsorship opportunities for Chamber programs and events, elevating your brand's recognition.
  • Access member-exclusive business development workshops and seminars to refine your skills and stay ahead of industry trends.
PRESIDENT ($CAD 400 / month)
  • Experience the ultimate membership package, incorporating all other membership level benefits, plus:
  • Premier recognition as a Chamber supporter at our signature events.
  • VIP access to exclusive networking receptions and business showcases.
  • Enjoy prominent logo placement on our Chamber's website and event materials, maximizing your brand's visibility.
  • Gain exclusive access to high-level networking events, connecting with influential community leaders and decision-makers.
  • Receive a dedicated business profile feature in our Chamber's newsletter, highlighting your achievements and success stories.
